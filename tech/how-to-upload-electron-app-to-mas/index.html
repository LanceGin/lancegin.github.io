How to upload your electron app to MAS

Aug 29, 2017 | tech | Hits

Before you read this article, make sure that you know how to build your electron app to a mas version with electron-builder.

You can look through the wiki of electron-builder, and that will help you to build your mas version.

But when you get your mas version, you can try to double-click the app, it will always be crashed, now you can follow the steps below to fix it.

  • Check your /build/mas/YOURAPP/contents, an embedded.provisionprofile should be existed, if not, you can download the file in Apple Developer.

  • After you adding the file to the specific path, add 3 files below to /build/mas/.

1.sign.sh

2.child.plist

3.parent.plist

this plist file will base on your app’s feature, you can find the security setting list in Apple Developer Center

  • Now you can use source sign.sh to package your mas version to pkg file, and the pkg file will be uploaded to MAS successfully
